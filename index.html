OVERVIEW

The MEST-hosted Africa Tech Summit is fast becoming the marquee technology event on the continent. Following the successful inaugural event in Ghana last year, ATS is proud to make its entry into Nigeria. Pulling in the brightest minds in the field, speakers include some of the biggest names in the tech and mobile space, including representatives from Google, Uber, Vodacom and Samsung, as well as a range of African entrepreneurs from leading companies Interswitch, Iroko, Main One and many more.
